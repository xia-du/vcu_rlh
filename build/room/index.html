Ackell Residence Center

Description

Bright, comfy private room, large queen size orthopedic mattress, wifi, emf blockers also, laundry 1 block away, cable, share large bathroom, welcome to bring groceries and use the kitchen appliances,etc to make meals. Heat and ac works great.

Hall Details

  • Contract: 9-month contract for freshmen
  • Demographic: Upperclassmen and freshmen
  • Hall Type: Apartment Style
  • Building Style: 4-story
  • Unit Style: 4-person apartments with single bedrooms
  • Capacity: 394
  • Dining plan: Not required

We are pleased to offer Philoto all on-campus residents. Philo allows you to watch live television on your own terms – on laptops and other devices anywhere on campus. With a stellar channel lineup, the ability to pause and rewind live TV, and 20 hours of your own DVR space, Philo gives you the flexibility and control you need to stay up-to-date on sports, current events, and the shows that matter to you most.

To watch Philo on a TV you will need a Roku or Roku TV or a laptop (with HDMI out) connected by HDMI cable to a TV (see supported devices). You can sign up to borrow a Roku from Residential Life & Housing for free by filling out The Roku Rental Form.
