Resident Assistant

Residential Life and Housing will be accepting applications for a limited number of anticipated openings for the position of Resident Assistant during the academic school year of 2016-2017. If you are interested in joining our team, please plan to attend one of the following information sessions:

  • Wednesday, November 16, 7:00pm in the Ackell Community Room
  • Tuesday, November 29, 9:00am in the Broad & Belvidere Community Room
  • Thursday, December 1, 8:00pm in the Cabaniss Community Room
  • Monday, December 5, 7:00pm in West Grace North, Room 1030J
  • Friday, December 9, 1:30pm in West Grace South, Room 1004A

The online application will be available beginning Monday, November 14, 2016 and will close on Thursday, January 19, 2017 at 11:59pm. It is REQUIRED that you attend one of the information sessions listed above in order to apply. A link to the application can be found here once the application opens on November 14, 2016. To apply, visit vcu.erezlife.com.

All submitted applications will be reviewed and selected candidates will receive an invitation to interview with us the weekend of January 27, 2017 – January 29, 2017. Offers will be made the week of February 13, 2017.

Please note that this announcement and application process is not for the Spring 2017 semester. This is only for those individuals interested in our anticipated vacancies for the 2017-2018 academic year.

If you have any questions, please feel free to email BeAnRA@vcu.edu.

We are pleased to offer Philoto all on-campus residents. Philo allows you to watch live television on your own terms – on laptops and other devices anywhere on campus. With a stellar channel lineup, the ability to pause and rewind live TV, and 20 hours of your own DVR space, Philo gives you the flexibility and control you need to stay up-to-date on sports, current events, and the shows that matter to you most.

To watch Philo on a TV you will need a Roku or Roku TV or a laptop (with HDMI out) connected by HDMI cable to a TV (see supported devices). You can sign up to borrow a Roku from Residential Life & Housing for free by filling out The Roku Rental Form.
